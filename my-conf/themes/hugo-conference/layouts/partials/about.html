The Midwest Women's Retreat is a 3-day Women’s Health and Healing Retreat, scheduled for Friday, June 28th through Sunday, June 30th, 2019 at King Conference Center, located at 6404 Woodland Ave, Kansas City, MO 64131.

Founder Gigi Jones, Certified Health and Wellness Coach and Kansas City native, has inspired women all over the world by healing herself from colon cancer through her message of self-love and let thy food be thy medicine.

Midwest Women’s Retreat mission: Our mission is to empower women, in various stages of their lives that are seeking professional and personal growth through mental healing, health and wellness and mentoring.

Midwest Women’s Retreat supports women in becoming healthy and whole. This support incorporates offering education to women who are seeking to transform their lives. Topics include lifestyle changes, stress reduction, embracing inner self through self-love practice, Healing from your past pain, Finding your inner happy spot, Your Heart has Secrets (Letting go) Healthy eating habits, Letting go of shame (shame no more),Yoga stretching exercises, Breath Work, Examine Loneliness (feeling redefine, Cultivating a cushion (financial freedom), and Meditation practice.

The goal of this year’s retreat is to offer workshops to women focused on healing by incorporating this mind, body, and soul approach.
