The Midwest Women's Retreat is a 3-day Women’s Health and Healing Retreat, scheduled for Friday, June 28th through Sunday, June 30th.

Founder Gigi Jones (Gigi The Vegan), Entrepreneur, Certified Health and Wellness Coach and a strong activist on women and children’s health. Gigi has inspired women all over the world by healing herself from colon cancer through her message of self-love and let thy food be thy medicine.

Midwest Women’s Retreat mission: Our mission is to empower women, in various stages of their lives that are seeking professional and personal growth through mental healing, stress reduction, health/wellness and mentoring.

The goal of this year’s retreat is to offer workshops to women focused on healing by incorporating mind, body, and soul approach.

Midwest Women’s Retreat supports women in becoming healthy and whole. This support incorporates offering education to women who are seeking to transform their lives.
